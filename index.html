Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

What are your support hours?

We provide 24/7 support through our online channels. Phone support is available Monday-Friday from 8am-8pm EST, and Saturday-Sunday from 10am-6pm EST.

How quickly can I expect a response?

Our average response time is 15 minutes for live chat, 30 minutes for phone calls, and 2 hours for email inquiries. Complex issues may require additional time.

Do you offer multilingual support?

Yes, our support team speaks over 10 languages including English, Spanish, French, German, Japanese, and Mandarin. Language selection is available in all our support channels.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my support plan?

Absolutely. You can change your support plan at any time through your account dashboard. Changes take effect immediately and are prorated for the remainder of your billing cycle.
